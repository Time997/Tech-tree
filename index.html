Tech Tree is a phone repair shop that specializes in fixing all types of phone issues. Our team of technicians is highly trained and experienced in repairing phones of all makes and models. Whether you have a broken screen, damaged charging port, or any other phone issue, we have the skills and equipment necessary to get your phone back to working order. In addition to phone repair, we also offer other services such as device unlocking, data recovery, and software updates. Our goal is to provide fast, reliable, and affordable repair services to our customers. We are dedicated to helping you get the most out of your phone and ensuring that it is in top condition.

Tech Tree was founded by Christiaan Overbeek, who has over 7 years of experience in the phone repair industry. His passion for technology and helping others led him to start Tech Tree, and he has been successfully repairing phones for customers ever since. Christiaan is highly skilled in all aspects of phone repair, from fixing broken screens to diagnosing and repairing complex issues. He takes pride in his work and is committed to providing excellent customer service. When you bring your phone to Tech Tree, you can trust that it is in good hands with Christiaan and his team of experienced technicians.
